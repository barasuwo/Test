A troll’s foot is a crime against nature. We're talkin’ yellow-green talons that could slice bread. Nails so long they double as back scratchers... for OTHER trolls. And don’t even get me started on the smell. It’s like someone left a cheese wheel in a dragon’s armpit for six summers. And the worst part? Trolls don’t wear shoes. Nah. And for the love of the swamp, stop using fungus as foot lotion. That’s not hygiene. That’s a war crime. Thank you. Now get outta my swamp."
